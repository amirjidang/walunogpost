Bayla Swid Missing: Family Pleads For Help In Locating Bartender

The news of Bayla Swid missing has left her family and friends devastated. They have been frantically trying to find her since she vanished on July 15, 2023. 

She worked as a bartender at Loch Bar in Boca Raton. Her colleagues reported that she didn’t show up for work, which was unusual since she was a responsible employee. 

Her family has been appealing to the public for help on social media and other platforms to locate her.

Last Known Location: Miami

According to reports, Bayla was last seen in Miami with a man who goes by the alias Robert Davis or Bradley Davis. 

The man is 44 years old and from California. Bayla’s coworkers are speculating that she may have gone on a trip with him, but it’s unclear whether it was voluntary or compulsive. 

She has not been active on social media, turned off her phone location, and has not contacted her family or coworkers since July 15.

The family is working relentlessly to find any information that could help locate Bayla Swid. 

They are appealing to anyone who may have seen her or her companion to contact them immediately or report to the authorities.

The Police and Their Investigation

As per reports, Bayla Swid’s family has filed a missing person’s report with the authorities. 

However, they have not been able to provide any concrete information or leads to assist in the investigation. 

The lack of leads and absence of help from the police has been frustrating for both Bayla’s family and the local community.

Bayla’s Coworker’s Appeal

Bayla’s coworker from Loch Bar in Boca Raton posted an appeal on Facebook. 

The post informed the public that Bayla was missing and that she was last seen with an unidentified man using an alias. 

The coworker also revealed that her last known location was in Miami on July 27, 2023, through a Samsung device. 

The post requested anyone with information to reach out to them or inform the authorities.

The Family’s Suspicions

Bayla Swid’s family is convinced that her disappearance was orchestrated by her companion, who has allegedly maxed out her credit cards, cleaned out her father’s joint bank account, and used her identity to open new credit cards. 

Bayla’s family believes that the stranger may have taken advantage of the situation to commit fraud. It’s still unclear whether Bayla was aware of the imposter’s intentions.

The Online Community’s Support

The online community has been empathetic and supportive of Bayla Swid’s family, rallying around them to help find their loved one. 

They have been sharing the news and appealing to their followers to join in the search. 

The family has been receiving messages of encouragement, which has been priceless for them in this trying time.

Law Enforcement and Public Awareness

Bayla’s family has expressed their gratitude to the local authorities who have been helping with the case. 

They are making an effort to inform people about the situation and how they can help by urging them to be on the lookout for Bayla Swid or her companion. 

They are also advising people not to deal with any suspicious or unusual credit card activities that may be linked to Bayla Swid.

Bottom line

Bayla Swid missing is a tragic incident that has rocked the community. Her loved ones hope that the collective effort in finding her will soon yield some results. 

They urge anyone with leads on her whereabouts to report to the authorities or contact the family. 

The family is appreciative of the unwavering support and promise to continue to update the public on any new developments.
