Who Is Juanpa Zurita Padres Fernando Martínez Zurita?

Fernando Martínez Zurita is the padres or father of social media star Juanpa Zurita. He was appointed as the political counselor at the Mexican Embassy in Washington, D.C., after which the whole family moved to the United States.

Martínez Zurita is married to his wife Teresa Arellano. He has four children, Fernando Zurita, Paola Zurita, Juanpa Zurita, and Andres Zurita. Juanpa often created vines and internet content with Andres.

All of Fernando’s children are very popular on Instagram. Juanpa has 28.1 million followers, Andrés has 2.1 million followers, Paola has 1.5 million followers, and Fernando has 57.9k followers on Instagram.

He was born in Mexico City, Mexico, but he was quite young when his family moved to the United States. Juanpa currently lives in Los Angeles with his parents and family. On his Instagram account, he calls himself a storyteller from Mexico to the world.

Meet Juanpa Zurita Parents: Who Is Mama Teresa Arellano?

Juanpa Zurita’s parents are very supportive of his internet career. His family frequently appears on his Youtube videos and vlogs.

Zurita’s mama Teresa Arellano often attends public events with him. They attended the GQ Men of The Year Awards 2016 at Torre Virrelles, Mexico City, Mexico, on November 9, 2016.

Are Juanpa Zurita Parents On Instagram?

Juanpa Zurita frequently posts pictures with his parents on Instagram. His padres, Fernando Martínez Zurita, has 57.9k followers on Instagram, and his mama Teresa Arellano is active on Instagram under the username @terearellanos.

Arellano has over 148k followers on the social media platform. Her Instagram bio says art and family. She also shares her official email id on the bio.

Is Juanpa Zurita Related To Humberto Zurita?

Juanpa Zurita and Humberto Zurita are not related to each other. The confusion began as they share the same last name, but the vlogger is not related to the Mexican actor, reports Republic World.

Humberto has ten siblings, and one of his sisters is named Teresa, which is the same name as Juanpa’s mother. However, his sister, Teresa, and Zurita’s mother, Teresa, are different people.
