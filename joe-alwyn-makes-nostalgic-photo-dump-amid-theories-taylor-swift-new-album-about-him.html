Joe Alwyn Makes Nostalgic Photo Dump Amid Theories That Taylor Swift's New Album Is About Him

Highlights

  • Joe Alwyn surprised fans with a return to Instagram after months of silence, sparking speculation about his private life and upcoming projects.
  • Taylor Swift's new album has fans wondering if it will include details about her past relationship with Alwyn, adding to the buzz surrounding the singer.
  • Swifties are eagerly awaiting the release of Taylor Swift's album, looking for clues and references to her ex-boyfriend Joe Alwyn in the tracklist and lyrics.

Seeing a new post on Joe Alwyn's Instagram is not as usual as one might think. It's been five months since the actor took a break from social media, and while he didn't delete his accounts or his previous posts, he wasn't posting anything either. It's not that strange, considering he has always been a private person, but after everything that's happened in his life, and around him, in the past few months, this ending to his social media hiatus came as a shock.

Taylor Swift has dated celebrities like Harry Styles, John Mayer and Calvin Harris, and her fans have plenty of thoughts about each relationship.

Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn's breakup was several months ago (or at least it was made public several months ago), but recently it's started making the headlines again as a new album by the singer is on its way. Swifties are convinced it will dive into both the relationship and the breakup, and with all these theories flying around, fans can't help wonder if Joe's sudden return to public life means something.

Joe Alwyn Returned To Instagram After A Long Break

Joe Alwyn's return to social media came as something of a surprise. The actor has always been known for being private, and after his breakup with Taylor Swift, he took protecting his privacy all the more seriously.

People all over the world were demanding answers about how, when, and why the relationship had ended, and save for a few comments from select sources, Joe Alwyn has remained completely quiet about the whole thing.

He also hasn't been spotted on any big projects in a while, and his return to public life, at least for a bit, has also led to speculation about whether he's got something up his sleeve.

The new post, which, like the rest of his Instagram, has the comments turned off, was a simple photo dump of what seem to be old memories. The whole post has a very nostalgic vibe, and the slides include a photo of his dog, black and white pictures of himself in important moments of his career, childhood pictures, landscapes, and a still of Ricky Gervais in The Office (UK) where he says "Different drinks for different needs".

Taylor Swift's Upcoming Album

Joe Alwyn's return to social media comes at a point where his past relationship with Taylor Swift is once again being put under the spotlight. Although Taylor is very famously dating Travis Kelce, it hasn't been that long since her and Joe's breakup, and swifties still wonder what went wrong in their six-year relationship.

When, during her acceptance speech at the Grammys, Taylor Swift announced the imminent release of her new album, The Tortured Poets Department, theories started going around about what the record would be about.

Fans are already theorizing that the album's title is a direct reference to Taylor's ex-boyfriend.

The singer revealed during the announcement that she had been working on this new album for two years and was finally ready to show it to the world, and fans took that to mean two things.

One, the album would dive into the final moments of the relationship. And two, things had been going badly for the former couple for a lot longer than people expected.

This last theory was cemented when, just days ago, the singer explained how lonely she felt while she was making her album Folklore. That was almost four years ago.

Also, when Taylor Swift released the track list for the new album, swifties couldn't help but find alleged references to Joe Alwyn. So, maybe Joe's return to social media has something to do with him wanting to share his side of the story when the album finally comes out.
