Inside Hailey Bieber's Modeling Career

According to Explore with Expert, Hailey Bieber began her modeling career at the age of 17, signing with Ford Models. Hailey's transition to modeling followed a childhood interest in ballet, which she had to give up due to a foot injury. Despite coming from a famous family, Hailey worked to establish her own identity in the modeling world.

Her early career saw her featuring in Ralph Lauren’s Denim & Supply line alongside Bella Hadid and began appearing in campaigns for major brands like Guess, UGG, and Levi's​​​.

According to Fashion Gone Rouge, Hailey's runway debut came when she went on to walk for high-profile fashion brands, including Tommy Hilfiger, Moschino, and Dolce & Gabbana.
