Pat Warren, a Former WJZ Anchor and Reporter, Died Unexpectedly

Maryland: Baltimore community Mourns Pat Warren, an Anchor and Reporter at WJZ for over three decades.

Pat Warren, a former anchor and reporter at WJZ for three decades, passed away unexpectedly over Thanksgiving weekend. Baltimore community mourns the celebrated journalist whose nearly 30-year career is marked by professionalism and excellence.

Who was Pat Warren?

Pat Warren was a former anchor and reporter for WJZ. She worked with the organization for nearly 30 years. She was from Canton, Ohio, and joined WJZ in 1992 from Texas. Her dedication and sharp reporting skills brought professionalism to the channel. She is best known for her coverage of Annapolis and the legislature.

She exhibited journalistic excellence with her informative reports delivered with clarity and incisiveness. She was committed to bringing truth to the people of Baltimore. Many have not hesitated to pay tributes to the late journalist, including former Maryland Governor Bob Ehrlich, who referred to Warren as “the consummate professional.” He said she was ‘universally trusted’ in the Annapolis. She reported clearly, without any agenda. She focused on reporting the truth and often did that with a sense of humor.

With her nearly 30-year career in journalism, she influenced the Baltimore news scene and inspired generations. Everyone respected her. All those who had the privilege to know and work with her will profoundly feel her absence.

What happened to Pat Warren?

The well-known journalist passed away over the Thanksgiving weekend. The details of her untimely death have not been made public. It is unclear what caused her sudden demise. Her former colleague, Mike Hellgren, announced her passing in a post on X (formally known as Twitter). The post read,

“Our former colleague Pat Warren has died. She was a no-nonsense journalist with a great sense of humor who served Baltimore viewers for decades as an anchor and political reporter at @wjz.

Rest in Peace, Pat.”

The Community mourns Pat Warren

The Baltimore community mourns its beloved journalist, who dedicated her life to enlightening the general public with information and truth. The Community has recognized her decades-long efforts and professionalism. They have paid homage to the late journalist and her hard work to honor her memory. They shared their fondest memories of her and the traits they admired the most about her: her commitment to the truth, her unparalleled reporting on politics, and her sense of humor. The city of Baltimore will deeply miss her. The world shall never see her like again.

We offer our deepest condolences to the family and friends. May she rest in peace.
