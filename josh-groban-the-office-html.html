Josh Groban Heading to The Office

Andy Bernard’s family keeps getting bigger and dorkier, but in a good way. The Office already cast Stephen Collins and Dee Wallace as the parents to Ed Helms’s character, but now they’ve found his brother: Josh Groban, your mom’s favorite singer turned occasional actor who’s been game to send up his image all of a sudden by playing a semi-douche in both Crazy, Stupid, Love and Glee (which cast him as a bizarro, New Directions–hating version of himself). Groban for office manager? [Inside TV/EW]
