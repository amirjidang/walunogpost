What Happened To Weather Woman Yanet Garcia And Why Is She So Famous?

While some rank celebrity status from the A-List to the D-List, there seem to be only two categories of fame nowadays. Firstly, there are the likes of Brad Pitt, Kendrick Lamar, Heidi Klum, Jerry Seinfeld, and Kim Kardashian, who have all dominated their respective industries. Whether you like their work or not, they are immediately identifiable and most people can name at least one of their credits or job titles. Then there are the celebrities who are ridiculously famous for more niche reasons. They aren't recognizable to the mainstream but they do have a dedicated fanbase. Yanet Garcia is absolutely in the latter category.
