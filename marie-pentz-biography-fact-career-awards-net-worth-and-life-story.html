Marie Pentz is a South African film and television actress. She is best known for her role as Mietie in the SABC2 comedy Koöperasiestories (1983-1985) and as Vera in Fishy Fêshuns (1999.)

Career

Marie Pentz made her acting debut in 1973, appearing as “Carmelita” in comedy film Die Spook van Donkergat.

After appearing as “Suzette Bosman” in Skooldae (1981,) “Hermiena Crump” in Miss Candida (1982,) “Magda” in Die Vlakte Duskant Hebron (1982,) she landed the recurring role of “Mietie” in SABC2’s hit comedy television series Koöperasiestories. She appeared in a total of 26 episodes staring from Intensies en bedoelings (April 1983) to Bring jou eie wewenaar (April 1985.)

Pentz was last seen in 2016, portraying the character of “Tan’Tilla” in Wimpie van der Merwe-directed short film Kolskoot.
