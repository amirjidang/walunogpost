Randy Houser Split: Country Singer to Divorce Wife Jessa Lee Yantz

Randy Houser will soon be single. The country singer and his wife Jessa Lee Yantz have decided to divorce after nearly three years of marriage, Us Weekly can confirm.

"I can confirm Randy Houser and Jessa Lee Yantz are getting a divorce," his rep tells Us in a statement. "They are appreciative of the media's sensitivity toward their request for privacy during this time."

Houser, 38, and singer-songwriter Yantz got engaged in July 2011 and tied the knot two months later in Punta Cana, Dominican Republic. The couple celebrated with an intimate ceremony, which included only 20 family members and close friends.

"I'm a good-hearted woman loving a good-hearted man," Yantz tweeted shortly before their nuptials. "Now that's a match made in heaven. Lucky in love."
