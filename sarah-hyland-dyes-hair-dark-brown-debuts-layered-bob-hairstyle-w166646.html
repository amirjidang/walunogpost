Sarah Hyland Dyes Hair Dark Brown, Debuts Layered Bob Hairstyle

"Hello from the darker side!" Sarah Hyland wrote on Instagram on Tuesday, March 8.

The Modern Family star, 25, debuted a complete hair makeover. Replacing her previously sun kissed highlights that she's worn since April 2014, Hyland went back to her naturally dark brunette locks. 

But color wasn't Hyland's only switch. She also took off major inches in a fresh haircut. The piecey, chin-length bob hairstyle is a dramatic change from her collar bone-grazing lob.

The Vampire Academy actress' new style comes just after completing filming on the latest season of Modern Family. Hyland showed off her old 'do while giving a shout-out to her costars, Ariel Winter and Nolan Gould. "And that's a wrap on season 7 for #modernfamily ❤️ The Dunphy kids," she wrote via Instagram. "See you for 8!"
