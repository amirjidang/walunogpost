Selena Gomez Nearly Trampled By Fans in Paris: Watch the Scary Video

They want what they want! Selena Gomez nearly got trampled while leaving Louis Vuitton’s show during Paris Fashion Week on Wednesday, Mar. 11.

The “Heart Wants What It Wants” singer — who sat in the front row with Dianna Agron and Chloe Grace Moretz — seemed to be in happy spirits posing for snapshots until she was quickly swarmed by fans and photographers. Her bodyguard took her under his wing and barricades were knocked down.

Gomez, 22, kept her head down during the fiasco, but appeared shaken up. After nearly two minutes trying to find her way through the crowd, the star eventually made it into her car.
