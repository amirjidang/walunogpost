Atwell called the rumors "invasive" and "upsetting," as they were "involving people in my actual life, my personal life, who have to be on the receiving end of that." Atwell became engaged to music producer Ned Wolfgang Kelly in April 2023. Hayley is said to have split from her British doctor partner in 2020 before filming the action movie with Tom.

She leaned on Cruise, who advised her to ignore the headlines. “When I’ve talked to him about it, he’d be like, ‘You know exactly who you are. You know what you’re about.
