Early Life

Sara Foster was born on February 5, 1981, in Los Angeles, California, United States, to parents David Foster and Rebecca Dyer. Her father is a music producer and her mother is a former model. She grew up alongside her siblings. She has a half-sister named Amy S. Foster.

Career

Foster started her career as a fashion model before she delved into acting.

She made her film debut in 2004 with a role as Amy Bradshaw in D.E.B.S. She followed up with a role as Nancy Hayes in the film The Big Bounce.

Foster came to prominence for her role as Jennifer “Jen” Clark in the television series 90210 and Roslyn Hanniger in the film Pysch 9.

She has starred in other films such as Demoted, Bachelor Party 2: The Last Temptation, and Barely Famous.

Personal Life

Sara Foster got engaged to tennis player Tommy Haas in 2010 and they have two daughters together, Valentina and Josephine.

She has dated the likes of Ashley Hamilton, David Spade, and Benicio Del Toro.
