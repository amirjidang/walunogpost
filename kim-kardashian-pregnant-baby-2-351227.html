Kim Kardashian And Kanye West Are Expecting Baby No. 2

After grueling Keeping Up With The Kardashians episodes of witnessing numerous failed attempts by Kim Kardashian to conceive her second child with Kanye West, the couple has finally gotten its wish, E! confirms. In the coming attractions for the second part of the show’s tenth season, the reality starlet reveals to her sister that her mission to make North West a big sister is accomplished.

“I just got the blood test back, and I am pregnant!” Kim tells Khloe in the short clip.

Kimye’s road to baby number two was paved with IVF treatments, biopsies and “having sex 500 times a day,” and the show chronicled nearly the entire journey. In the mid-season finale, Kim even turned to a psychic for positive reinforcement. Candid about the process in interviews and the like, Mrs. West was admittedly having less fun the second time around.

“We’re literally trying everything that we can to make it happen, so I’m to the point now where it’s not as stressful to me,” she said. “And maybe that’s when they say it happens.”
