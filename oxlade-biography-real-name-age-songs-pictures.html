Oxlade Biography: Real Name, Age, Songs &amp; Pictures

Oxlade whose real name is Ikuforiji Abdulrahman Olaitan is a Lagos based Nigerian singer and songwriter born on April 22, 1997.

Oxlade was raised by his grandmother, having lost his mom at a very tender age.

He is a versatile singer with music genres of Afro-beat and Afro-pop.

Career

Oxlade had developed passion for singing while in school.

He came through with his debut EP “Oxygene”.

He came to limelight with his hit single “Away”, the lead song off the ‘Oxygene’ EP. The song was accompanied with a lovely visual as directed by the talented Cinematographer, TG Omori.

His numerous hit releases has captured the heart of music lovers.

He has worked with the likes of Reekado Banks, Melvitto, DJ Tunez and so on.

Oxlade collaboration with grime acts, Skengdo, Sneakbo and AM has got him audience from a diverse group.
