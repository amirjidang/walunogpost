Scholarship Criteria

The Max Emerson Champions Find a Way Scholarship evaluates applicants based on the following criteria:

  • Demonstrated resilience and the ability to overcome adversity

To apply for the Max Emerson Champions Find a Way Scholarship, interested senior wrestlers in Kentucky must submit the following:

  • Personal statement describing their wrestling journey and how they have overcome challenges
