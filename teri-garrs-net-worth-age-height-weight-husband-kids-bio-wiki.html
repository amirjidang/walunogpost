Early Life

Teri Ann Garr was born in Lakewood, Ohio, on 11th December 1944. Her father, Eddie Garr, was a performer, comedian, and actor in the vaudeville genre. Her mother, Phyllis Lind Garr, was a stage actress and model. Being surrounded by stage performers, she soon discovered her passion for dancing. She had two elder brothers, Ed and Phil Garr.

Teri Garr lost her father when she was 11 years old. Since then, her mother supported her. She received her education from North Hollywood High School, situated in Northridge, and spent most of her time practicing ballet. She mastered other forms of dance and worked as a dancer in Elvis Presley movies.

Personal life

In 1993, she married a contractor, John O’Neil, when their adopted daughter Molly O’Neil was born. Unfortunately, Teri had to divorce in 1996.

Career

During the initial days of her career, Teri worked in commercial advertisements. She made several guest appearances in Television shows like Star Trek and That Girl.
