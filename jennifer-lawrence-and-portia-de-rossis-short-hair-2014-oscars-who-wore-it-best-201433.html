Jennifer Lawrence and Portia de Rossi Wear Same Short Hair at Oscars

Jennifer Lawrence and Portia de Rossi both made waves at the Oscars with their short hairstyles. The two ladies amped up the volume of their 'dos and swept back their blonde tresses.

De Rossi, 41, wore a sexy beaded Naeem Khan gown and revealed that her wife since 2008, Ellen DeGeneres, had been feverishly preparing for the big night. "She actually won't allow me to see anything until tonight," De Rossi told E!.

Lawrence, who was nominated for American Hustle but lost to Lupita Nyong'o, wore a stand-out red Dior Haute Couture gown with a peplum.
