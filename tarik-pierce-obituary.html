Who Was Tarik Pierce?

Tarik Pierce was a seasoned HR and training and development professional who spent most of his career in Washington D.C. 

He was born and raised in Florence, South Carolina, and later pursued his studies in Human Resource Management and Development at Clemson University. 

Tarik was an advanced tech prodigy who always kept up with the latest technological advancements and applications in the field.

Tarik began his career as an HR & Training Officer at the Bureau of Economic Analysis, where he honed his skills and developed a reputation for his attention to detail and ability to work well with others. 

In 2017, Tarik joined the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs as a Curriculum and Competency Manager, where he continued to make a positive impact through his innovative solutions and collaborative approach.

Tarik’s sudden and unexpected passing came as a great shock to his family, colleagues, and friends, who are still grappling with the reality that they will never see his charming smile or hear his infectious laughter again.
