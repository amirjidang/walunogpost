Owen Wilson's Relationship With His Daughter Might Be Strained, But What's He Like With His Sons?

For almost 30 years, Owen Wilson has been entertaining audiences with his acting skills, but there is one role Owen Wilson plays that not many people know about: Dad. Although he is most recognized for his work in high-profile comedies, he has also made a name for himself in rom-coms and dramas. Wilson's early career began with small roles in films such as Bottle Rocket and The Cable Guy, but it wasn't until his breakout performance in the hit comedy Zoolander that he became a household name. Since then, he has starred in a variety of films, from the family-friendly Night at the Museum franchise to the darkly comedic Inherent Vice.

Recently, Wilson debuted in the film Paint as a Bob Ross-inspired character, once again leaving audiences excited to see what other memorable performances he will bring in the future. Despite his success, Wilson has remained grounded and is known for his easy-going personality both on and off-screen. His relationship with his children, though, has fallen under scrutiny.

How Many Kids Does Owen Wilson Have?

Owen Wilson, a widely recognized bachelor in Hollywood, has three children. Although he is not romantically involved with any of his children's mothers, Wilson maintains a close relationship with his two sons, Ford and Finn. Ford was born in 2011 to Jade Duell, while Finn was born three years later to fitness professional Caroline Lindqvist.

Then, according to People, in 2018, Wilson welcomed his third child, Lyla, with Varunie Vongsvirates. Despite the unconventional family dynamic, Wilson revealed in an interview with Esquire that he and his children's mothers have a good relationship and everyone gets along well.

Does Owen Wilson See His Sons?

Actor Owen Wilson has a strong bond with his two sons, 12-year-old Robert Ford (known as Ford) and 9-year-old Finn. Wilson has spoken openly in interviews about how much he values his role as a father and treasures his relationship with his children. It's clear that Wilson takes his parenting responsibilities seriously and is dedicated to being a loving and supportive dad.

Although Owen Wilson and the mothers of the two boys make every effort to keep them out of the spotlight, numerous pictures of Wilson and the boys engaging in various activities together have been taken over the years.

What Happened With Owen Wilson And His Daughter?

Despite his financial support, Owen Wilson has been noticeably absent from his daughter's life, leaving many to wonder why. The Internet is awash with theories as to why the actor seems to have a problem connecting with Lyla. While some speculate that Wilson's busy work schedule may be to blame, others suggest that there may be deeper personal issues at play. Whatever the reason, it is clear that Wilson's absence has left a void in his daughter's life.

Due to Wilson's growing love and respect for his sons, Varunie finds it challenging to remain a distant observer anymore. Despite dating for five years, Varunie and Wilson have lost touch entirely, leading to Owen not communicating with their daughter. Speculations arise amongst fans; some believe that Varunie intentionally got pregnant without his knowledge, knowing full well he did not want any more children. Others suggest that she was trying to force him into marriage; however, even if they did not tie the knot, he would be equally involved in Lyla's life as he is with his sons.

Varunie has made her battle to get Owen Wilson to meet his daughter very much public knowledge, which in turn has led to his mental health battles coming back as a topic of concern for many individuals. The actor has struggled with depression for years, and in 2007 attempted to take his own life. Although briefly mentioned at the time, this information has been kept hidden from the public eye.

What Is Next For Owen Wilson?

As mentioned, Owen Wilson took on a new role in his latest film, Paint, as a public television painter named Carl Nargle. His character boasts a unique signature look with his perm hairstyle and customized van and has gained a loyal following of fans who closely watch his brushstrokes. However, when a younger and more talented artist takes everything and everyone that Carl loves, he is forced to confront the reality that there is more to life than ratings on television. Written and directed by Brit McAdams, Paint is a humorous portrayal of the cutthroat world of two artists vying for the title of the next Bob Ross on television.

Also, coming soon Disney's famous Haunted Mansion attraction is getting a live-action adaptation, with Owen Wilson set to star as the leading role. Wilson will portray Kent, a priest hired by Gabbie (Rosario Dawson), a single mother who has been experiencing paranormal activity in her newly purchased New Orleans mansion. Unbeknownst to her, the house has a dark history and was sold at an unusually low price. It will be interesting to see how Disney turns this eerie attraction into a compelling movie experience.
