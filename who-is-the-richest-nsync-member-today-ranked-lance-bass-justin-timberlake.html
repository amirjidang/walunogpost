NSYNC Members Ranked From Richest To Poorest

Highlights

  • Justin Timberlake is the most successful member of NSYNC, with a net worth of $250 million. He has had a successful solo career and also pursued acting.
  • Lance Bass has a net worth of $20 million and has been involved in various business ventures. He is also a philanthropist and recently became a father of twins.
  • JC Chasez has a net worth of $16 million and has focused on his solo career as a songwriter and producer. He also remains positive about his time with NSYNC and cherishes the harmony they achieved.

Once upon a time in the late 1990s and the 2000s, NSYNC was a name that no one could escape. The musical collective, which consisted of Justin Timberlake, JC Chasez, Chris Kirkpatrick, Joey Fatone and Lance Bass, rose to fame thanks to their self-titled debut album. Singles like I Want You Back,Bye Bye Bye,It's Gonna Be Me, Girlfriend, and others propelled the boy band's name as one of the most important cornerstones of pop culture.

However, those glory days are long gone. The boys recorded their one last tune as a collective back in 2002 before going into an indefinite state of hiatus. Since then, they each have embarked on their own respective solo careers, albeit reuniting for several special occasions. To celebrate their impressive career, here's a look back at the members of NSYNC, what they're up to now, and a definitive ranking based on their net worth, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Update September 2023: Since NSYNC went on hiatus, each of the individual members has remained busy both with their personal and professional lives.

Most recently, it was announced that Justin Timberlake will be releasing his latest album, a FutureSex/LoveSounds 2.0 of sorts at the top of 2024. He is also waiting for his latest film, Reptile to be released. But with the WGA and SAG-AFTRA strike, there is no telling when that will be.

Lance Bass is remaining busy as the father of twins and the host of the successful podcast, Frosted Tips. All the business ventures that Bass has been involved with over the years helped him make his fortune, as NSYNC made him and the rest of the group household names but not rich.

JC Chasez has been busy working on shopping around two musicals that he wrote. He also partnered with Meow Mix for a cat food campaign that is keeping his name fresh in the minds of business executives and hopefully opens the door for other opportunities Chasez wants to explore.

Joey Fatone has not only had to learn how to navigate through a divorce and co-parenting but deciding what he wants his career to look like moving forward. And for him, that is potentially doing more work behind the camera.

Chris Kirkpatrick is best known for hitting the 90s Con and Pop Paradise 2000 circuits. The stories that he tells with members of other boy bands are not only entertaining but nostalgic for both Kirkpatrick and the audience alike.

5 Justin Timberlake's Net Worth Is $250 Million

Justin Timberlake is undoubtedly the most successful member of NSYNC. After the group, he went on to make his mark as a solo artist and became one of the best-selling musicians of all time with ten Grammy Awards under his belt. This helped him to build his fortune of $250 million.

He also went off to pursue an acting career and most recently finished Grant Singer's feature-film directorial debut in the crime thriller Reptile.

The father of two boys, whom Timberlake shares with Jessica Biel chooses to be very hands-on with his kids. But with both his acting and singing career and Biel's acting and producing career, the two feel fortunate that they are able to rely on their nannies to help raise their boys as far out of the spotlight as possible.

4 Lance Bass' Net Worth Is $20 Million

In addition to his singing career, Lance Bass has a few lucrative business lines under his name. He formed an entertainment company Lance Bass Production in 2007 and announced its partnership with Logo in the same year. A certified cosmonaut, he planned to join the TMA-1 mission to the International Space Station before his financial sponsors backed out back in the years. Though with a net worth of $20 million, he may have been able to pay for his portion of the trip himself.

The wealth and the glam of Hollywood don't seem to phase the crooner. He's been involved in a plethora of philanthropic causes over the years, most notably when he honored his younger cousin, who has Down's syndrome, back in 2003 by donating $30,000 to establish the Amber Pulliam Special Education Endowment.

Since NSYNC disbanded, Bass has gotten married to long-time partner, Michael Turchin and the two welcomed twins via a surrogate, Alexander and Violet in 2021.

3 JC Chasez's Net Worth Is $16 Million

Before NSYNC, JC Chasez was a member of The Mickey Mouse Club alongside his future band member Justin Timberlake, as well as Ryan Gosling, Britney Spears, Christina Aguilera, and Felicity star Kelly Russell. After NSYNC, JC continued elevating his solo career as a songwriter and producer, most notably for acts like Sugababes, David Archuleta, Basement Jaxx, and more.

His debut electropop-blended album as a solo artist, Schizophrenic, was released in 2004 after a series of unfortunate delays. It nonetheless added to his net worth of $16 million.

While some members of pop groups that have gone their separate ways can be bitter about it, Chasez instead looks back fondly on his days with NSYNC.

"We knew we wanted to be a harmony group from the very beginning, and it started with that idea," Chasez explained. "So, it was actually very organic. We were all focused on making a great sound, so it’s like if your voice fits in this range, that’s where you’re going to live in the band. It felt natural that way."

2 Chris Kirkpatrick's Net Worth Is $10 Million

Years after departing NSYNC, Chris Kirkpatrick had a few minor acting and voice-over roles in movies and television series over the years. He notably provided his distinctive singing voice for pop star Chip Skylark on Nickelodeon's The Fairly OddParents and competed in some of the most exciting game shows, like Gone Country on CMT in 2008 and his recent endeavor, Celebrity Big Brother, in February 2022.

"I think I'm least looking forward to not seeing my family or having that connection with the outside world that I usually have," the boy band legend told EW about his recent endeavor with Big Brother. "It's going to be difficult being cut off from everything and not even being able to hug my son every morning or kiss my wife every morning or things like that."

Kirkpatrick welcomed a new addition to his life, a son, from his marriage to his longtime partner Karlyn Skladany in 2017. He still makes music, at least back in the early 2010s as the frontman of alt-rock band Nigel's 11, helping to build his net worth to $10 million.

1 Joey Fatone's Net Worth Is $7 Million

In addition to his singing portfolio as an NSYNC member, Joey Fatone is also a talented dancer. He put his feet to the test in 2007 by coming in second place on ABC's Dancing with the Stars. An avid fan of cooking, he also hosted Food Network's Rewrapped and My Family Recipe Rocks on Live Well Network. As such it is no wonder he is worth a cool $7 million.

Recently, he announced his new line of calzones at Schlotzsky's sandwich and pizza franchise. This doesn't mean he has stopped singing though, as he tied with Rumer Willis on season one of The Masked Singer in 2019. While Fatone has done some smaller-scale directorial work, he has said that his next project will be a documentary. What the details of that documentary are, have yet to be revealed.

But what about an NSYNC reunion? Is that in the cards?

"As far as NSYNC is concerned, never say never, but I know right now that nothing's happening," Fatone explained. "No talks about it, because everybody's been doing their own thing. Chris is on 'Big Brother' right now. Lance has two kid twins. JC's been writing and producing. Justin's been out writing and producing."

Perhaps one day the group will come back together. But for now, fans have to remember the group in its heyday and reminisce about what was.
