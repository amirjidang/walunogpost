Bonnie Francesca Wright was born on 17th February 1991 in London, England. She is an English actress, fashion model, screenwriter, director, and producer. Bonnie Wright has a current estimated net worth of $12 million.

She is known for playing the role of Ginny Weasley in the Harry Potter film series. It has not been very difficult for her to build her wealth after the huge success of the Harry Potter films. This young actress of 22 years has already appeared in two TV movies as well. In the movie “Stranded” that was released in 2002, she plays the role of the younger version of Sarah Robinson. In the movie “A Life in Pictures” that was released in 2004, she plays the role of the younger version of Agatha Christie.

Bonnie Wright has an elder brother, Lewis who urged her to audition for the role of Ginny Weasley for the Harry Potter movies. Her brother claimed that the character reminded him of his own sister. Wright’s parents are jewelry designers. She obtained her primary and secondary education in North London. Wright started attending London's University of the Arts during the filming of “Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows”. Her plan is to continue acting and at the same time work behind-the-scenes.

Bonnie has several offers and is currently working on many movie projects and modeling assignments. She has been a successful model as well and has won the title of the Most Edgy Look at the 2011 Rodial Beautiful Awards. She was dating Jamie Campbell Bower, a British actor who was seen playing the younger version of Gellert Grindelwald in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows. They were supposed to get engaged in April 2011, but called off their engagement on 30th June 2012.
