Jacqueline Laurita was born on April 20, 1970, in Las Vegas, Nevada, USA. She has an estimated net worth of $500 thousand. She derived most of her wealth from acting career. She studied at Cross high school in Las Vegas before her acting career commenced in 1990. She has appeared in several reality TV shows including The Real Housewives of New Jersey. She eventually became the main actress in the reality show. She has won three Emmy awards in year 2003, 2004, and 2006 as the best female reality show actress.

She married Matt Holmes in 1989. The couple had one child they named Ashley but they divorced in the year 1992. In the year 2002, she married Chris Laurita, and together, they have two children named Christopher “CJ” Laurita and Nicholas Francis Laurita.
