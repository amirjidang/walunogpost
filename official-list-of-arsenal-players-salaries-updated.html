Current List of Arsenal Players Salaries Per Week 2018

This post will list the wages that Arsenal Players earn this 2018. It will also answer the question as to who is Arsenal’s highest paid player and which players are the most paid at Arsenal Football club. Prior to their signing of Alexis sanchez, Petr Cech, Mesut Ozil, Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang and Lacazette, the wage bill of the Gunners rose from £166 million to around £180 million. They signed some players and also improved the contracts of some of their football players.

Arsenal Players Salary Per Week

Arsenal in recent times are known for disappointing their fans as they have not lived up to expectations. No wonder they have been criticized for lack of signing quality players. This outcry prompted manager of the football club, Arsene Wenger to sign the likes of Henrikh Mkhitaryan since Alexis Sanchez moved to Manchester United. With this reinforcement, he can face the likes of the top clubs in the Premier League Chelsea, Liverpool and Manchester City.

Note that to keep the likes of their top players, the club pay big wages to these players in the Premier League yet the Londoners have not won a Premier League trophy for the past 10 years. You can compare their salaries to Chelsea players weekly wages.
