Did Aileen Wuornos Kill Her Brother Keith Wuornos?

No, Aileen Wuornos did not kill her brother Keith Wuornos. He died of esophageal cancer on July 17. She received $10,000 from his life insurance after he died.

Aileen and Keith grew up while remaining in touch but unfortunately he got diagnosed with cancer. She did not murder Keith in a Pink Flamingo Motel room because of blackmail from his side in 1976.

Moreover, Keith was only 21 years old when he passed away in Michigan. As per reports, there were no signs of foul play, let alone evidence of a shooting that proves that the soon-to-be serial killer didn’t murder him.

Aileen Wuornos List Of Victims Explored

Aileen Wuornos’s list of victims is very long. She murdered seven men within a period of 12 months.

Wuornos’s first victim was Richard Charles Mallory who was 51 years old and was the owner of an electronics store in Clearwater. She killed him on November 30, 1989. She claimed that she killed him in self-defense when he attempted rape.

Aileen then killed David Andrew Spears who was a construction worker. His naked dead body was found on June 1, 1990, and was shot six times. Her next target was Charles Edmund Carskadon whose dead body was found on June 6, 1990, with nine bullet shots.

Aileen further murdered Peter Abraham Siems who was a retired merchant seaman he was missing and his body was never found. Then the next victim was Troy Eugene Burress whose body was found on August 4, 1990, with two shots.

The serial killer killed Charles Richard “Dick” Humphreys whose body was found on September 12, 1990, with six shots in the head and torso. 

Wuornos last targe was Walter Jeno Antonia. His naked body was found on November 19, 1990, in Dixie County. He was shot four times.
