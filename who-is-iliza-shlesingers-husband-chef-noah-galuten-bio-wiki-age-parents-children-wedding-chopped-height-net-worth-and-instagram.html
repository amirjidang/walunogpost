Noah Galuten Bio – Noah Galuten Wiki

Noah Galuten is an American writer and also a chef who came into the limelight when he got married to his wife Iliza Shlesinger a popular American comedian. Her popularity has greatly impacted on her husband and this has helped to expose him to a wider audience around the United States. He has also worked in different places and engaged in different things before he met his wife and got married.

He schooled at Santa Monica High School in California. He later proceeded to the University of California in Los Angeles where he studied theatre and graduated in the year 2004. After his graduation, he moved places to New York to try playwriting but he was unsuccessful and therefore did not proceed with the same.

After quitting playwriting, he ventured into food blogging known as “Man Bites World.” He then traveled around the US to learn of different cuisines of different countries as he wrote in his blog. This attracted a large audience to his website with time.
