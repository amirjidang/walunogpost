What She Say? Kelly Rowland One Of My Beauty Icons Is Oprah Winfrey

One of my beauty icons is Oprah Winfrey. I love that fact that she’s so comfortable with herself. One of my favorite episodes of her show was when she showed millions of people how she woke up in the morning with no makeup on. That was so selfless and revealing. She’s very real and genuine and I love that. Halle Berry is one of the most beautiful women I’ve ever seen. Same thing with Whitney Houston and Janet Jackson — especially as a kid, because they have brown skin like me. I remember looking at magazines and television and seeing someone that looked like me and seeing a piece of myself in them.

Kelly Rowland is stunning! The debate can rage on, and she can be compared to several timeless beauties, but she will always be a cocoa-skinned bombshell in our book. Inside and out, she seems as real and as genuine as the Halle Berry’s, Oprah Winfrey’s and Janet Jackson’s of the world. With her no. 1 hit “Motivation” climbing the charts, her newly appointed position as a judge on X-Factor and looking flawless almost at every glance, is it safe to say that Miss Kelly is about to start commanding the attention of the U.S. like no other?

We think so.
