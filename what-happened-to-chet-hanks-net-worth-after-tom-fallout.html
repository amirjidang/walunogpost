What Happened To Chet Hanks' Net Worth Since Tom Cut Him Off?

Tom Hanks has been called by many fans and critics as one of the greatest actors of all time. Any role he is given, even if the movie doesn't have praising reviews, he has managed to excel with his charismatic approach. In his first marriage with the late Samantha Lewes, he has two children, with Colin taking on acting after his father. When he married his current wife Rita Wilson, they welcomed two sons into the world, including his son Chester "Chet" Marlon Hanks. Chet also took on acting, but has been focusing as a musician, releasing his viral video "White Boy Summer" in 2021 as a play on Megan Thee Stallion's "Hot Girl Summer."

Chet has become somewhat of the black sheep in his family, due to the controversies he was associated with, whereas his father has the phrase "America's dad" given his clean and nice persona. While it is good for Chet to make a name for himself, it certainly hasn't really been an entirely positive experience. With Chet being financially cut off from his parents, how has this affected him?

How Chet Feels About His Parents

One of the questions Chet got a lot from his followers is simply, "What's it like to be Tom Hanks' son?" This question stuck out to him ever since he was 14 and went to the White House while George W. Bush was president. The 43rd president asked the young Chet, "“Hey Chester, what’s it like growing up the son of a famous father?” The 14-year-old at the time, caught off guard, replied, "Well Mr. President, there’s a lot of advantages, but sometimes it can be pretty weird." In response, Bush chuckled back with "That's a good answer. I think I know a little something about that." This gave Chet and Bush a common feeling of being famous for what their father was recognized for.

Chet goes on to say that for the opportunities he had, he was blessed for having them and also loved his parents. According to Chet, fame is the most powerful drug to humanity and the most destructive. For recognition he would get just for being the son of Tom Hanks, it gave contempt and make people assume that he's spoiled rotten, but he claims that he's privileged, not spoiled. If he were to want money, he wasn't just simply given it, but instead his father told him to do simple chores and he'll pay him for it, earning the money in the process.

Aftermath Of Tom Cutting Off Chet Financially

As of 2022, reported by Idol Net Worth, Chet Hanks has an estimated net worth of around three million dollars. He seems to be doing well even going through a lot of hardships, including his struggles with substance abuse and his complicated history with his ex-girlfriend Kiana Parker, who is the mother of his daughter Micaiah.

Even if Chet isn't under his parents' name in finance, he's made it clear that he loves his parents and he's grateful to grow up as someone who could have had everything he wanted with the fame and fortune, but he has learned to be humble with what he's given and achieve things on his own.

What Is Chet Up To Now?

Today, Chet has been content in what he is currently doing. While he has endured of what other people have said behind his back or take advantage of him due to being the son of Tom, the 31-year-old overcame the anger and the insecurity he felt when growing up as a privileged, but mostly normal kid growing up. By creating his YouTube channel, Chet is using it to be open and honest about himself and giving himself a spotlight that has nothing to do with how he got to where he is. Instead, he prioritizes in doing his best to be a better version of himself.

Chet's personal life seems stable, as he has a girlfriend named Melissa Mays, who is also an artist. They have posted loving tributes on Valentine's Day, cementing their dedication and love for one another. His website Hanxfit is where he hosts his fitness program and is one of the ways he gets his income. Even with some questionable statements he made in the past and the controversies he was in, Chet is more than just a son of a famous actor. With his willingness to improve physically and emotionally, he is putting himself on a stable path that has been beneficial for him so far.
