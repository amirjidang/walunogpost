Ini Edo Biography

Ini Edo is a Nigerian actress and producer who came to limelight after portraying the role of Ulima in ‘World Apart’ a romantic comedy movie, that featured Kenneth Okonkwo, Hilda Dokubo among other Nollywood stars. Beyond the cameras, the beautiful actress is an entrepreneur and philanthropist.

Ini Edo Early Life

Ini Edo was born on 23rd April 1982 in Akwa Ibom, Nigeria. She is the second born in a family of four, one elder cousin that she considers her elder sister, a younger brother and a younger sister. The Nollywood goddess was brought up in a strict and religious background.

People might confuse her pretty face with a daughter of a Nigerian Tycoon but trust me on this, the beautiful actress understands every challenge that any young Nigerian woman goes through because she went through a bitter experience growing up.

Ini Edo attended Cornelius Connely College then proceeded to the University of Uyo where she graduated with a Diploma in Theatre Arts. The beautiful actress also attained a Degree in the English Language.

Ini Edo Career

Ini Edo began her acting career back in 2000 when she went for auditions which were organized by a popular Nollywood producer where she performed very well and was offered her first role in a movie.

The beautiful goddess performed so well in her first movie ‘Thick Madam’ and was advised by the producer and several other people to relocate fully to Lagos where she would become a full-time actress. Unfortunately, her father opposed the idea because he felt that the actress was too young to go out on her own and pursue a career away from home.

Luckily, her father gave in and Ini Edo ventured fully into what she knew best, and that was acting becoming one of the popular and beautiful actresses in Nollywood. The curvaceous actress has featured in more than two hundred films since then.

Some of the popular movies she has featured in include, Tears of Nancy, Royal Gift, Ghetto Queen and so many others.Ini Edo has also produced several movies such as ‘I’ll Take My Chances’ alongside Emem Isong which was an instant hit and has been graced on screens across Nigeria.
