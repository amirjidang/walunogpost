What Happened To Dolph Lundgren's First Wife?

Throughout most of history, fans who wanted to know where they knew an actor from had two options, ask the other people in the room and rack their brains trying to figure it out. These days, however, fans can simply go online to find the answer to their question in minutes.

Given the fact that most people have an infinite amount of information available to them in their pocket at any given moment, it can be frustrating when the definitive answer to a question can't be found online.

Unfortunately for Dolph Lundgren's fans who are interested in his personal life, his marital history is debatable.

According to several websites including hollywoodmask.com and hollywoodlife.com, Lundgren married a woman named Peri Momm and they divorced shortly thereafter.

Those same reports say that Momm completely eschewed the spotlight so nothing is known about what she has been up to since divorcing Lundgren.

While it may be unsatisfying to not know what Lundgren's first wife is up to now, there is a fascinating twist in the story of the movie star's early-'90s marriage. After all, it seems entirely possible that Lundgren never married a woman named Peri Momm at all.

While several websites claim Dolph Lundgren got married in 1991, several others have no reference to that. Most notably, Wikipedia and whosdatedwho.com both have no reference to Lundgren marrying a woman in 1991.

Unless Lundgren confirms or denies he got married to Peri Momm in 1991, it seems like that will remain a mystery.

What Happened To Dolph Lundgren's Wife Anette Qviberg?

Unlike the mystery of Dolph Lundgren's reported first marriage, there is no debating whether he walked down the aisle in 1994. After all, that was the year that Lundgren married a woman named Anette Qviberg.

After dating for just short of two months, Dolph Lundgren and Anette Qviberg got engaged. Together for a long time, the couple's marriage lasted just short of 17 years after they walked down the aisle.

Who Has Dolph Lundgren Dated?

Since Dolph Lundgren became famous in 1985, he has only been single or married for a fraction of that time. During the rest of the time, Lundgren has been linked to several women.

Some of the women that Lundgren has been involved with over the years aren't well known today. On the other hand, Lundgren reportedly dated two memorable models in the past, Grace Jones and Janice Dickinson.

Most recently, Lundgren began dating a Norwegian personal trainer named Emma Krokdal and they got engaged in 2020. Unless something has changed and they haven't said anything about it, Lundgren and Krokdal remain together to this day.

When news broke that Lundgren and Krokdal were engaged, most people remarked on their age gap. The reason for that is Krokdal is a staggering 39 years younger than Lundgren.
