The Highest-Grossing Cillian Murphy Movies, Ranked

Looking to explore the dazzling world of box office success and mesmerizing performances? Well, look no further! We've got the scoop on the 10 highest-grossing movies starring the incomparable Cillian Murphy, ranked for your reading pleasure. From thrilling action flicks to gripping dramas and everything in between, Cillian Murphy has left an indelible mark on the silver screen with his captivating performances and undeniable talent. So, get ready to be amazed as we take you on a journey through Murphy's top-grossing films, in which he has wowed audiences and earned his rightful place among the Hollywood elite. Let's count down the list and see which of Murphy's masterpieces comes out on top!

In the Heart of the Sea - 2015 ($89.6 Million)

Via Flickr/Rojgar Aur Nirman

Murphy sets sail in In the Heart of the Sea, a 2015 historical adventure drama that grossed $89,693,309 at the box office. In the film, Murphy shines as Matthew Joy, a crew member aboard the ill-fated whaling ship Essex. Despite its modest box office earnings compared to other films on this list, Murphy's performance as the resilient and resourceful sailor is noteworthy. His portrayal adds depth to the film's gripping survival tale, showcasing his ability to bring authenticity to his characters.

Red Eye - 2005 ($96.6 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In this suspenseful thriller, Murphy takes on the role of Jackson Rippner, a smooth-talking assassin who terrorizes Rachel McAdams' character. With a global box office gross of almost $97 million, Murphy's performance as the charming yet menacing Rippner is a highlight of the film. He effortlessly balances the character's calculated coldness with his own innate charm, creating a believable and thrilling performance that keeps the audience on the edge of their seats.

Transcendence - 2014 ($103 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Murphy's performance as Agent Buchanan, an FBI agent investigating a rogue artificial intelligence, in this futuristic sci-fi thriller is nothing short of transcendent. His portrayal of the skeptical and persistent investigator adds a layer of intrigue to the film's complex narrative, as he delves into the ethical implications of advanced technology. Murphy's dedication to the role shines through, adding depth and nuance to his character, and ultimately contributing to the film's success at the worldwide box office with a gross of $103 million.

In Time - 2011 ($165.1 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In this dystopian action thriller, Murphy plays Raymond Leon, a timekeeper tasked with enforcing the strict time-based economy. Murphy's portrayal of the ruthless enforcer adds tension to the film's high-stakes plot, making it an instant classic. His character's motivations and actions drive the narrative forward, and Murphy delivers a compelling performance that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats. Despite its mixed reviews, the film's worldwide box office gross of over $165 million is a testament to Murphy's ability to captivate audiences.

Cold Mountain - 2003 ($165.1 Million)

Via Flickr/Archer Avenue

Murphy's versatility as an actor shines through in his role as Bardolph, a Confederate soldier, in this epic period drama. His portrayal of a troubled and conflicted soldier grappling with the horrors of war adds emotional depth to the film's narrative. Murphy's performance is both tender and powerful, capturing the complexities of his character with authenticity. His contribution to the film's success is evident, as it grossed over $165 million worldwide, making it a commercial hit and showcasing Murphy's skill in bringing compelling characters to life on the big screen.

A Quiet Place: Part II - 2021 ($296.6 Million)

Via Flickr/Tribun

Murphy joins the cast as Emmett, a fellow survivor in a post-apocalyptic world plagued by deadly creatures in this sequel to A Quiet Place. Murphy's portrayal of Emmett is gripping and intense, capturing the internal struggles and unwavering determination of his character with his trademark authenticity. His performance adds depth and suspense to the film, elevating the overall narrative and contributing to its global box office success, grossing over $296 million worldwide. Murphy's compelling presence on screen is a standout in this thrilling sequel, showcasing his ability to captivate audiences.

Batman Begins - 2005 ($358.8 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Murphy's portrayal of Dr. Jonathan Crane, aka the Scarecrow, in this gritty reboot of the Batman franchise is a standout performance. His eerie and sinister depiction of the fear-inducing villain adds psychological depth to the film, elevating the overall narrative with his nuanced acting. Murphy's ability to captivate audiences is on full display as he brings the Scarecrow to life with his mesmerizing performance. His portrayal of the character is a memorable highlight in this story of the Dark Knight, contributing to the film's commercial success, grossing over $358 million worldwide.

Dunkirk - 2017 ($512.3 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In Christopher Nolan's gripping war film Dunkirk, Murphy delivers a compelling performance as a shivering soldier, capturing the fear and desperation of the harrowing evacuation of Dunkirk during World War II. His role adds to the film's emotional impact, resonating with audiences as they witness the horrors of war through his character's eyes. Murphy's ability to convey the intense emotions of his role is a standout moment in this epic war film, which grossed over $512 million worldwide. His remarkable performance left a lasting impression in this critically acclaimed film.

Inception - 2010 ($728.5 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In Christopher Nolan's mind-bending sci-fi thriller Inception, Murphy portrays Robert Fischer, Jr., a wealthy heir and the target of a complex heist. Murphy's performance as the conflicted Fischer is captivating, as he navigates through the layers of dreams and realities. His portrayal adds depth to the film's intricate narrative, showcasing his ability to portray complex characters with finesse. Murphy's performance contributes to the massive success of the film, which grossed over $728 million worldwide, making it a box office hit.

The Dark Knight - 2008 ($1 Billion)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Murphy's portrayal of Dr. Jonathan Crane, aka the Scarecrow, in The Dark Knight, a superhero film is a memorable standout. His sinister and menacing performance as the fear-inducing villain elevated the tension and suspense in the film. Murphy's ability to captivate audiences with his nuanced acting is evident in his portrayal of the Scarecrow, contributing to the massive success of the film, which grossed over $1 billion worldwide. His remarkable performance left a lasting impression in this critically acclaimed film.
