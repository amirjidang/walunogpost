What Happened To Gabe Rygaard Of Rygaard Logging? Obituary And Death Cause Of Ax Men Cast

Gabe Rygaard was a reality T.V. star best known for his role in the History channel’s T.V. series “Ax Men.” Unfortunately, Gabe died in a fatal car accident.

Rygaard first appeared on “Ax Men” in its second season in 2008 and remained on the show until his death. After his father decided to retire, Rygaard took over the chair at Rygaard Logging that same year.

What Happened To Gabe Rygaard Of Rygaard Logging?

Gabe Rygaard, a reality T.V. star, was killed in a three-vehicle crash on U.S. Highway 101, a quarter-mile east of Laird Road, west of Port Angeles, at 9:22 a.m.

Craig, his father, confirmed his son died in the crash. According to Clallam Fire District No. 2, Assistant Chief Dan Huff, the driver of a Ford Bronco, died at Olympic Medical Center due to injuries sustained in the crash.

Huff said that seven people in three vehicles were injured in the crash. The others received minor injuries and were treated at the scene.

Gabe was later identified as the Bronco’s driver by the State Patrol. According to the State Patrol, Gabe’s1995 Ford Bronco was traveling east in the second lane of U.S. Highway 101 near milepost 243, behind a black Chevrolet Tahoe.

The report said that the Tahoe began to slow down, and his Bronco veered to the left and collided with the back of the Tahoe.

The Bronco spun out and collided with a Dodge Ram van traveling west in the opposing lane. Rygaard was not wearing a seat belt.

According to Huff, eleven personnel from District 2 were dispatched, along with an engine, two ambulances, and a command vehicle. Highway 101 was closed until around 3 p.m., and traffic was detoured near Laird Road.
