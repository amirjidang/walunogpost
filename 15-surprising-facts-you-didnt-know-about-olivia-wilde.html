13.  She Married An Italian Prince At 18

Growing up in a world full of celebrities and other influential people can often lead young girls in Hollywood to make rash decisions. In 2003, Olivia Wilde married Tao Ruspoli, an Italian prince. The two of them eloped in a crazy ritual on a school bus when she was only 18-years-old. There were only a few witnesses that attended their school bus nuptials. The Ruspoli family owns a castle in Vignanello, Italy where they had their formal wedding later on. In 2011, Wilde filed for divorce citing “irreconcilable differences.” Wilde did not ask for spousal support, and the divorce did not get ugly like most Hollywood unions. One such Twitter follower posted, “@oliviawilde Pregnancy is not an illness or disability. You're rich, call Uber or catch a cab! Entitled much?”

Olivia Wilde has stated that she had always been obsessed with Spielberg movies and meeting Spielberg had been a dream of hers.
