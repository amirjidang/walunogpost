Noah Johnson Missing Ohio: The Truth Behind His Disappearance

Noah Johnson went missing in Ohio in the third week of November 2022, and he was last seen or heard from on the weekend before Thanksgiving. 

Eight harrowing weeks have passed since Noah Johnson went missing in Bryan, Ohio. He was reported missing on November 19th, 2022. 

According to Kaitlynn Johnson, Noah’s sister, he was seen at home on November 17th, 2022. 

The following day when Noah’s wife returned home around 10:00 a.m., she found a note written by Noah on the table, and he was nowhere to be found. 

The note mentioned Harrison Lake. Anxious about Noah’s whereabouts, his wife immediately went to Harrison Lake to look for him. 

Noah’s car, wallet, and jacket were found in the parking lot at Harrison Lake. His family moved the vehicle from there, hoping Noah would see his car gone and return.

Since then, multiple attempts have been made to find Noah. 

Numerous agencies are continuously looking into his disappearance, including the Bryan Police Department, the Ohio Department of Natural Resources, Fulton County Sheriff’s Office, Toledo Fire and Rescue Dive Team, Michigan State Police Dive Team, State and County K-9 teams, volunteers, family members, and non-profit search organizations.

Noah’s family members are desperately looking for a clue or answers. They’re offering a $5,000 reward for information that leads to him.

“So, we just wake up all the time thinking about it. It’s always on our minds,” said Noah’s father, Pat Johnson.

Noah, 22, was happily married before he disappeared. According to the authorities, Noah’s wife has continued cooperating and is not considered a suspect in Noah’s disappearance. 

In a news release, the police also wrote, “Verbal and written statements indicating self-harm heightened concern regarding Noah’s well-being.”

Noah’s family has been giving out brochures throughout the city to find him. In the flyer, his family has mentioned his physical features, the date he went missing, a $5000 reward for anyone that finds him, and his picture. 

His family has also been sharing pictures of his 2012 Chevy Malibu car to see if anyone might have spotted it the weekend before Thanksgiving. 

They’re also sharing his gamer handle, “Ungrateful_Frogs,” and information regarding all recent online games he played to see whether anyone may have interacted with Noah before he disappeared. 

What could be the reason behind Noah’s disappearance? 

Neither his family nor any law enforcement agency has found a reason for Noah’s disappearance. 

The details of the note Noah left at home have not been disclosed, but according to the investigators, some aspects of the letter indicate self-harm.
