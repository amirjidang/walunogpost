    Kendo Kaponi Legal issues

    Kaponi was first arrested at the age of thirteen, on a drug charge, gun law violation, attempted murder, and liberty restriction. He was released when he was nineteen. In 2014, Kaponi was arrested for domestic violence, and given bail. In 2016, he was also detained because his car was not up-to-date with its transfer process, and in January 2017, Kaponi was detained for contempt on an alimony order.

    After two years in prison, Kendo was released and interviewed by Primer Impacto, where he claimed he had hit the man because the man had injured his son and not to rob him. Kaponi was arrested by federal agents in 2018 on charges of armed burglary and assault. He was arrested on PR-143 in the barrio of Hayales, Coamo, Puerto Rico, and extradited to Florida.

    He was charged with assaulting barber, Jon Erik Baez-Molano, at the Custom Kuts establishment in Winter Haven, Florida on July 28, 2017, with a metal pipe and a piece of wood.
