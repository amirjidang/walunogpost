NLE Choppa Offers Fade To Blueface In Celebrity Boxing Match

NLE Choppa is done with the Twitter fingers and lyrical jabs. The Memphis artist offered the fade to Blueface and is interested in the two going at it in a celebrity boxing match.

Notably, the Los Angeles artist was the original aggressor in the situation. He called out Choppa and his baby’s mother in JaidynAlexis’ “Barbie (Remix)” which dropped on Wednesday (Jan. 3), rapping about how she wants to have sex with him. He then took things to X, formerly known as Twitter, writing, “Ni**as rap about my wesydes all year long and mad when I rap about his… She decided to indulge in the circus on her own free recognizance she entered the chat nobody safe. I’m not worried about a Muslim vegan cryp on sY bet that small fry… squabble up or shut up.”

He had a few more tweets after, doubling down on the fact the “Sl*t Me Out” artist’s baby mother wanted to have sex with him, claiming that he “put lil bro on,” and then wondering why he wasn’t getting any responses.

NLE Choppa finally responded hours later, writing, “Let’s box @bluefacebleedem who can set up celebrity boxing match in Vegas me and him hit me ASAP.” Choppa then hit him below the belt, virtually, alluding to Blueface’s abusive relationship with Chrisean Rock, claiming that he is “undefeated vs women.”

Their tension runs deep as Marissa Da’Nae, Choppa’s baby mother, first accused the Famous Cryp rapper of trying to get with her months ago. Rock brought it up last month during her latest back-and-forth with her ex-boyfriend, claiming he was unsuccessful in his attempt because he has “no [rizz].”

It is unclear who was flirting with whom, but it seems Choppa and Blueface are ready to settle things in the ring. The “Thotiana” rapper has been successful in his short amateur boxing career. Last year, he defeated Ed Matthews by TKO and he defeated Kane Trujillo in 2021.
