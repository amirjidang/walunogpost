Coffee Joulies Pitch on Shark Tank

Coffee Joulies made their memorable pitch on Season 04 Episode 13 of Shark Tank USA. How Coffee Joulies Work

Coffee Joulies are innovative stainless steel beans designed to revolutionize the way we enjoy our coffee. These beans are not your ordinary coffee accessory – they are embedded with a phase-change material (PCM) that allows for precise temperature control.

When placed in a hot cup of coffee, the PCM inside the Coffee Joulies absorbs the excess heat, rapidly cooling down the beverage. This process ensures that your coffee reaches the ideal drinking temperature quickly, preventing any burnt tongues.

Once the coffee reaches the desired temperature, the phase-change material solidifies, effectively trapping the heat within the Coffee Joulies. As a result, the stored heat is gradually released back into the coffee, effectively maintaining the perfect temperature for a longer period.

By harnessing the principles of heat absorption and release, Coffee Joulies provide a consistent and enjoyable coffee-drinking experience. Whether you prefer your coffee piping hot or lukewarm, these innovative beans allow you to savor each sip at your desired temperature.

The Science Behind Coffee Joulies

At the heart of Coffee Joulies is the incredible power of phase-change material. This material has the ability to absorb and release large amounts of heat without undergoing a significant temperature change. The PCM inside the Coffee Joulies functions as a thermal battery, providing controlled heat management.

When the Coffee Joulies are exposed to a hot beverage, the phase-change material transitions from solid to liquid state, absorbing the excess heat and cooling down the coffee. As the coffee cools, the PCM solidifies again, slowly releasing the stored heat and maintaining the optimal temperature.

This ingenious mechanism allows Coffee Joulies to keep your coffee warm for extended periods, ensuring that every sip is as enjoyable as the first.
