Travis Kelce Denies Hes Serious About Taylor Swift By Insisting Theyre Just Having Fun

Highlights

  • Travis Kelce describes his relationship with Taylor Swift as "nothing but fun" and emphasizes their enjoyment of each other's company.
  • Kelce reflects on their journey together, highlighting how he introduced Swift to the football world and how she has been a dedicated supporter of the Kansas City Chiefs.
  • Taylor Swift attends the Super Bowl with her famous friends, including Blake Lively, and is seen congratulating Kelce on the field with sweet moments and words of affection.

Swifties are growing increasingly eager to know Travis Kelce’s intentions in dating Taylor Swift, with engagement rumors continuing to plague the lovey-dovey couple. However, Travis didn’t give a clear answer to speculation when he referred to their romance as simply “fun.”

In an interview published on Sunday, February 11, just ahead of Super Bowl LVIII, Kelce, 34, opened up to CBS Sports’ Tracy Wolfson about his relationship with Swift, describing it as "nothing but fun."

Travis Reflected On His Relationship With Taylor Thus Far

Reflecting on their journey together, the Kansas City Chiefs tight end expressed, "We’ve both been learning about this lifestyle," highlighting how he introduced Swift, 34, to the football world during the 2023 season. Swift attended her inaugural NFL game in September 2023, when they first hard launched their romance after beginning to date in the summer, and has since been a fervent supporter, attending over 10 Chiefs games. Travis, too, has made appearances at her ongoing Eras tour stops, though he was a no-show at the recent Grammys while prepping for the Super Bowl.

During Sunday's preshow interview, Travis shared about his connection with Swift, describing it as "an unexpected ride" that he has thoroughly enjoyed. Expressing his excitement for the impending Super Bowl, where he would later celebrate his third championship with the Chiefs (with a beaming Taylor in the crowd), Travis mentioned how even Coach Andy Reid has embraced Swift's presence among the team's cheering squad, indicating that everyone is having "some fun" with it.

Reflecting on the ups and downs of the 2023 season, Kelce characterized it as "a fun roller coaster" that he wholeheartedly embraces. He noted that the 2023 squad faced more challenges than in previous years, yet they persevered to make it to Super Bowl LVIII, where they successfully faced off against the San Francisco 49ers. With anticipation building for the big game, Kelce teased, "We’ll see where the cards end up," a statement that aged well, considering he did bring home the trophy to Taylor.

Taylor Watched The Super Bowl With Her Famous Friends In The Stands

The Eras hitmaker was in the stand with several famous members of her squad, including Ice Spice, Keleigh Sperry, and Blake Lively, who was reportedly adorned in nearly $500,000 worth of jewelry to accessorize her red and white tracksuit.

For her part, Taylor kept her look simple with a black, monochromatic outfit, which reports say she spent around $50,000 in total. Taylor’s jewelry increased the price by a bit, but her pieces were clearly carefully chosen, as several were references to her NFL star boyfriend.

During the commotion, cameras caught Lana Del Rey take a tumble while sitting close to Swift. She appeared to lose her balance as the crowd got rowdy following a big play. Footage that’s now gone viral on social media shows Taylor not noticing her friend’s fall at first, though those around Lana quickly stepped in to help once her distress was noticed. The singers were able to shake off the brief incident, and Taylor was later photographed on the field after the Super Bowl congratulating Travis with several sweet moments and words of affection.
