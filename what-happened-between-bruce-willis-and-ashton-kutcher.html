Bruce Willis' Beautiful Friendship With Demi Moore

When Demi Moore and Bruce Willis broke up, they cited irreconcilable differences as the reason. It was reported that the primary reason for their split was because Willis wanted Moore to be a stay at home mom while she was at the height of her career.

Since Demi Moore wasn't willing to give up her acting career, their marriage would eventually lead to divorce. The two share three kids Rumer, Scout, and Tallulah, who are now great friends and enjoys a blended family together.

Despite their split, Willis admitted that he still loves Demi Moore. During his Rolling Stone interview, he said, “I still love Demi. We’re very close. We have three children whom we will continue to raise together, and we’re probably as close now as we ever were. We realize we have a lifelong commitment to our kids. "

Their relationship is now motivated by the friendship they made while co-parenting their three kids. The actor continued, "Our friendship continues. The institution has been set aside … It’s difficult to live your life and marriage under a magnifying glass, which is what happens to movie star couples.”

Bruce Willis Was Apparently Furious That Demi Moore Made Peace With Ashton Kutcher

An insider to Ok Magazine revealed that Willis is now happy with the way things are and he apparently, "doesn’t want to see Ashton back in the mix”. The source further continued “Bruce doesn’t get why she’s willing to play nice when Ashton’s done nothing but humiliate her.”

Bruce Willis Was Upset That His Daughters Stayed In Touch With Ashton Kutcher

Willis was also furious that his children stayed in touch with Kutcher. Ashton Kutcher and Demi Moore reportedly got in touch again after Moore went to film an ad with Kutcher's wife, Mila Kunis. Despite Moore and Kutcher's divorce, the Die Hard star's daughters have continued their connection to the No Strings Attached actor.

This upset Willis even more as the source even claims, “Bruce isn’t thrilled that the girls still talk to the guy and that they gave their stamp of approval to Demi doing the ad with Mila. But Ashton was a dream stepdad to them, and that only aggravates him even more.”

Ashton Kutcher is close with Demi Moore's daughters, and they created a strong bond with each other. During Ashton Kutcher's guest appearence on Marc Maron’s podcast, he said, “I make a really conscious effort to stay in touch with the girls. I love them, and I’m never going to stop loving them, right? And respecting them and honoring them and rooting for them to be successful in whatever they’re pursuing.”
