Young Jeezy Lists Atlanta Penthouse For $1.1 Million

Rap star Young Jeezy, last seen fending off a copyright lawsuit from Brian Smith back in April, is back in the news again today after it emerged that he has listed his swanky Atlanta penthouse condo for sale.

The 3,140 sq. ft., double story, 3 room and 3.5 bathroom unit is listed for sale for a cool $1.1 million, representing an over $200,000 drop from the former Black Mafia Family member’s original purchase price of $1.32 million in 2007.

Constructed from stone and a stucco hardcoat, the fully furnished unit features a 20-foot high ceiling, hardwood floors, a huge walk in closet, two large balconies, a professionally landscaped garden and motorized thermal-paned windows.

The luxurious penthouse, which offers a gorgeous view of the Atlanta skyline, is located on the top floor of the Phoenix On Peachtree in Buckhead. Occupants of the six floor building enjoy 24 hours security, a door man and concierge services.
