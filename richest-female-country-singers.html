Kacey Musgraves Net Worth $10 Million

Kacey Musgraves was born on August 21, 1988, in Golden, Texas and she is an American country singer and songwriter. She has won the grammy award for Best Country Album in 2018. Kacey Musgraves began songwriting as a child at the age of 8 years old.

This only goes to show her love and passion for music. Her music is unique as it has talked about topics that are far from ordinary that listeners are used to such as love, friendship, heartbreak and growing up. Musgraves has written songs that speak out about LGBT acceptance, and marijuana use.
