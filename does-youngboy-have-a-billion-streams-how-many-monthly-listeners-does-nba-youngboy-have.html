Does YoungBoy have a billion streams? How many monthly listeners does NBA YoungBoy have?

NBA YoungBoy, born Kentrell DeSean Gaulden, is a rising star in the rap scene who has captured the hearts of fans and listeners around the world. Known for his impressive body of work and loyal following, the Baton Rouge native has been making waves in the music industry. In this article, we’ll delve into two significant milestones that highlight his influence: his extraordinary streaming numbers and the impressive number of monthly listeners on Spotify.

YoungBoy’s Music Journey

Before we dive into YoungBoy’s streaming achievements, let’s take a moment to appreciate his journey in the world of music. The 24-year-old rapper made a name for himself between 2015 and 2017 by independently releasing eight mixtapes, gradually building a dedicated following of fans. In August 2017, he took a monumental step in his career by signing with Atlantic Records. His music resonates with audiences across the globe, marking him as a noteworthy figure in the contemporary rap landscape.

Billion Streams Milestone

One remarkable achievement that underscores YoungBoy’s impact is the fact that he has surpassed an astonishing 8 billion streams on Spotify across all credits. This remarkable feat speaks to the rapper’s popularity and the widespread appeal of his music. His catalog of songs has consistently attracted listeners, amassing a substantial number of streams on the popular music streaming platform.

Impressive Monthly Listeners on Spotify

In addition to his billion streams milestone, YoungBoy boasts an impressive number of monthly listeners on Spotify. According to reports, his Spotify account has reached more than 20 million monthly listeners. This places him in an elite category of artists who have an enormous and dedicated fan base regularly tuning in to his music.

NBA YoungBoy’s music journey has been nothing short of extraordinary. His ability to connect with fans through his music is evident in his incredible streaming numbers and the remarkable number of monthly listeners he has on Spotify. Surpassing 8 billion streams on the platform signifies the widespread appeal of his music while having over 20 million monthly listeners speaks to his consistent and ever-growing popularity.

As YoungBoy Never Broke Again continues to make his mark in the rap world, his streaming numbers and monthly listenership reflect not only his talent but also the deep connection he has with his fans. His influence is set to continue to grow, making him a notable figure in contemporary music.
