Darcey &amp; Stacey: Why Aren't Stacey Silva's Sons On The Show?

When it comes to twinning, 90 Day Fiancé stars Darcey and Stacey Silva have their act down to a science! In fact, both of the stars have two children each, with Darcey having daughters and Stacey having sons, and they are all around the same age.

In the second season of the show, viewers are got an inside look into Darcey's rocky relationship with Georgi, and things were unraveling fast. Despite the duo feeling the heat, all eyes were on Stacey and Florian as the two discussed growing their family.

In fact, fans thought at one point that Stacey and Florian were "relationship goals," despite the negative rumors about Florian's intentions.

Family seems to be quite a big issue when it comes to the duo, leaving fans a little confused as Stacey already has two sons, however, they're never on the show. Although Darcey Silva has her daughters on, viewers wonder why Stacey's boys are nowhere to be found.
