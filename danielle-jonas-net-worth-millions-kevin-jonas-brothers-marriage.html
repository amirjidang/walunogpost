How Danielle Jonas Amassed A $7 Million Net Worth

Danielle Jonas is an American reality TV star, actress, and entrepreneur who is best known for being the wife of Kevin Jonas, the oldest member of the pop-rock band The Jonas Brothers. Danielle Jonas (née Deleasa) was born on September 18, 1986 in Denville, New Jersey. She attended Morris Knolls High School and later pursued a career in cosmetology. Danielle was a hairdresser in her early days, building a loyal clientele. She later met hubby Kevin Jonas while vacationing in the Bahamas in May 2007 before getting married on December 19, 2009. The pair is parents to two lovely daughters, Alena Rose, born on Feb. 2, 2014 and Valentina Angelina, on Oct. 27, 2016.

It's easy to chalk up Danielle Jonas' status as a public figure to her being married to Kevin Jonas, but she's much more than a Jonas wife. In fact, Danielle has worked hard to build quite an impressive net worth for herself. While she may be overlooked because she's the least famous Jonas wife, Danielle has made herself successful enough to stand beside Nick Jonas' sweetheart Priyanka Chopra and Sophie Turner, whose impending divorce from Joe Jonas has recently made headlines. From humble beginnings as a hairdresser to her current status as a successful public figure, here's how Danielle Jonas as amassed an impressive $7 million net worth.

7 Danielle Jonas' Reality Television Stint On 'Married To Jonas'

Danielle Jonas' venture into the realm of reality television could be seen as the first contributing factor to her now-impressive net worth. Jonas gained popularity when she co-starred on the E! reality-documentary series, Married to Jonas, alongside her husband Kevin Jonas from 2012-2013. The couple's reality TV series premiered on E! on Aug. 19, 2012, and went on to run for two seasons. The series began following the couple two years into their marriage, just as they were settling into their suburban New Jersey home.

The show brought Danielle to the spotlight alongside Kevin, establishing her as not only a hairdresser from New Jersey, but also, a reality star. Her earnings from Married To Jonas undoubtedly contributed to Danielle Jonas' net worth. Jonas was actually asked if she would like to join the cast 'Real Housewives of New Jersey' and politely refused. “I was asked,” she said. “But I think I would die. I think they would like, they would kill me.”

“I’d rather do Married to Jonas,” added Danielle, referencing her and Kevin’s reality show.

6 Danielle Jonas' Net Worth Grew With Other Television Ventures

Danielle Jonas' stint on Married To Jonas was far from the end of her journey on the small screen. In addition to the reality series, Jonas has also appeared on The Rachael Ray Show alongside hubby Kevin many times, specifically a segment titled "Dinner & Drinks with Kevin & Danielle Jonas", "Kevin & Dani Jonas' Marriage Secrets" and another time when she and Kevin offered Rachael Ray their cooking tips in the kitchen.

Danielle also managed to snag a reoccurring guest host role on Fashion News Live from 2012-2015. While her appearances on television beyond Married To Jonas might be limited to a few, it has no doubt added to her net worth and further boosted her fan base.

5 Danielle Jonas Launched Her Jewelry Line

Danielle Jonas' net worth continued to increase when she released her own custom jewelry line, Danielle Jonas Co. Jonas launched her namesake brand in 2018 and has released three collections since then: Moments (comprised of nameplate necklaces and pieces with birthstones), New Beginnings (filled with dainty necklaces, earrings, and rings), and Perla (consisting of designs with links and diamonds).

Jonas said that her grandmother inspired her decision to start the line. "My inspiration has always come from my grandmother's jewelry and how she always had pieces that would transition from day to night so seamlessly, so when I'm designing I try to emulate her style that always inspired me," she explained to Insider.

"It's something I've always had a passion for, and I love having something to call my own that I can focus my creativity on," she added.

4 Danielle Jonas Partnership With Dreft

In late 2013, when Danielle and Kevin Jonas were expecting their first daughter, Alena, the pair decided to partner up with baby fabric detergent Dreft. With Dreft, the couple launched an app called Amazing Baby Days that allows couples to capture all the moments from their pregnancy through the first year of a child's life. It was the perfect opportunity for Danielle Jonas' net worth to grow, as she used her partnership with the company to reveal the long-anticipated birth of the couple's first daughter.

Dreft even live-tweeted Alena Rose's birth, and posted the first public photo of the baby with her mother, Danielle Jonas, on the brand's Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram feeds.

3 Danielle Jonas' Net Worth Increased After She Released Her First Children's Book With Kevin Jonas

Danielle Jonas continued to dip her hands in other ventures to increase her net worth, with the 2022 release of a children's book titled "There's a Rock Concert in My Bedroom". Danielle and husband Kevin were inspired by their daughters to release the book that highlights the power of music and family — and is illustrated by Courtney Dawson. "Music has always been a foundational creative outlet in our family," the couple said in a joint statement they shared exclusively with PEOPLE.

"Now our daughters, Alena and Valentina, enrich our lives in ways we could have never anticipated," the stars continue. "The love and care they show each other on a daily basis inspired this story, and we are so proud to share how these girls not only uplift us and each other, but are creative, caring rock stars in their own right.".

2 Danielle And Kevin Jonas Release Second Children's Book

Following the success of "There's a Rock Concert in My Bedroom", Danielle Jonas partnered up with hubby Kevin again to release another book in 2023 titled "There’s a Beach in My Bedroom". The “imagination-focused picture book” was deemed a bestseller and follows a family of mom, dad, and two girls who, following the disappointment of changed plans, conjure a day at the beach from the comfort of their own bedroom.

With two successful children's books under her belt, Danielle Jonas' net worth has definitely benefited.

1 Danielle Jonas' Partnership With Sanofi And Regeneron

An unfortunate struggle prompted Danielle Jonas to partner with Sanofi and Regeneron. Danielle recalled feeling uneasy and shying away from doing things she enjoys, like supporting her husband Kevin Jonas on tour, when she first noticed signs of moderate eczema on her scalp years ago.

Danielle recently partnered with Sanofi and Regeneron to teach people more about Dupixent, a treatment for people 6 months and older with moderate-to-severe eczema that was recommended by her doctor. Danielle Jonas' net worth grew with her partnership with pharmaceutical companies, while also helping her to resume her life comfortably in the spotlight. “I just wanted to tell people about it because it helped me so much,” she explained in an interview with HollywoodLife. “It just got me my life back a little bit more.”
