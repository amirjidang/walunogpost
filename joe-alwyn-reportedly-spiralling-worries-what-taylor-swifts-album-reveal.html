Joe Alwyn Is Reportedly Spiralling As He Worries What Taylor Swifts New Album Will Reveal

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift fans rejoice as she announces her 11th studio album, expected to reveal intimate details of her personal life.
  • Joe Alwyn, Taylor's ex-boyfriend, is reportedly concerned about what the album will reveal and how it could affect his career.
  • Speculation arises that the album's title, "The Tortured Poets Department," may be a reference to Joe, based on a previous interview.

Taylor Swift fans are elated with the news that the pop star is going to release her 11th studio album later this year. A lot has happened in Taylor’s personal life since she graced the world with new music, from her break-up with Joe Alwyn after six years of dating to her new high-profile romance with Chiefs player Travis Kelce.

Joe Is Worried About What Taylor’s Songs Will Say About Him

But it’s not only fans who are wondering what Taylor will reveal on her new songs, given that she’s known for writing about her personal life, especially relationships. Apparently, Joe Alwyn is growing increasingly worried about what her album could reveal and how the public’s response could hurt him and his career.

“It’s safe to say that Joe is bracing himself for a lot of questions. No one is safe,” a source who’s allegedly close to Taylor disclosed to Life & Style . “Joe should be worried about what she reveals.”

Taylor revealed new music is on its way during the 2024 Grammys ceremony on Sunday, February 4 while accepting the Best Pop Vocal Album, marking her 13th Grammy triumph.

"The way I can celebrate is by telling you a secret that I’ve been keeping from you for the past two years, which is that my brand new album comes out April 19th," she told the crowd during her acceptance speech. "It’s called The Tortured Poets Department and I’m going to go post the cover right now backstage."

Fulfilling her promise, Taylor promptly turned to Instagram, where she shared the album's cover art, featuring her reclining on a bed adorned in black lingerie. Alongside the visual, Swift unveiled what appeared to be handwritten verses, possibly hinting at new lyrics that’ll be featured on the highly anticipated record.

The Name Of Taylor’s Album Seems To Reference Joe

Some fan theories suggest the name of Taylor’s upcoming album is a reference to Joe. Following Swift’s announcement, social media sleuths unearthed an interview from December 2022, where Joe did an interview with Paul Mescal for Variety's Actors on Actors segment, where he revealed they have a group chat they dubbed "The Tortured Man Club,” a name eerily similar to Taylor’s new album’s title.

“It hasn’t had much use recently,” the Conversations With Friends star admitted in the interview. Paul, 28, agreed as he chimed in, “No. I feel like we’re less tortured now.”

Taylor and Joe reportedly began dating in late 2016, shortly after her highly publicized breakup with Tom Hiddleston. Alwyn is known for his roles in movies such as "Billy Lynn's Long Halftime Walk" (2016), "The Favourite" (2018), and "Mary Queen of Scots" (2018). The pair’s break-up was announced early last year. Swift had a brief romance with The 1975 frontman Matty Healy before debuting her relationship with Travis Kelce in September, though she’s since revealed they began dating earlier in the summer before taking their romance public.
