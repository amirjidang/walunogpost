Khloe Kardashian Leaked Unfiltered Photo Fiasco

Khloe Kardashian’s leaked unfiltered photo has reportedly caused her a lot of distress and embarrassment. The 38-year-old star’s team is going above and beyond to get the image removed from the internet. 

In the viral photo, Khloe Kardashian can be seen wearing a leopard print string bikini while standing beside the pool. Khloe’s grandmother, MJ, clicked the leaked image. 

A source close to the famous family told The Sun that Khloe “completely lost it” and wanted it taken down “as soon as possible” when the post was shared. 

The Kardashians’ crisis management team has desperately attempted to remove the snap from the internet entirely. The team is sending “legal threats” to take down the bikini shot. 

Despite all the efforts, the leaked unfiltered photo keeps getting more viral. People don’t seem to get enough of the leaked bikini shot. 

The informant said there was a “miscommunication” between the KUWTK star and her team about “what was supposed to be posted at the time.”

“She was freaking out and within minutes the photo already began circulating everywhere,” the source said.

The informant claimed that Khloe felt nervous and embarrassed because she knew this photo leak would be a big deal. 

Now that the photo was out in the open, many people were genuinely shocked to see this rare unedited photo of Khloe, while many others appreciated how beautiful she looked. 

Despite the compliment, it was reported that the youngest Kardashian sibling is quite pissed at being under the microscope whenever a photo of hers is posted on the internet. 

Some people went as far as claiming that Khloe looks completely ‘unrecognizable’ on Instagram. 

The 36-year-old star knew that her leaked unfiltered photo would only make matters worse for her. 

No comment was made from Khloe’s side, but some sources claimed that she was dying to come out and speak in public. 

Two years after the leaked photo fiasco, Khloe has shared some unfiltered bikini pictures on her terms. Taken by sister Kendall Jenner, the images show Khloé posing side-on in front of a doorway, her body bathed in natural light, some cellulite on display.

Khloe’s pic showed her wearing a small triangle bikini top and matching string thong bottoms, and the silvery-gray matching set accentuated her glowing skin.

Her bold move has been appreciated by many people and is considered a significant step towards self-acceptance. 

Exploring the career of Khloe Kardashian

Khloe Kardashian is the youngest of the famous Kardashian siblings. She is an American media personality and socialite. Since 2007, she has starred with her family in the reality television series Keeping Up with the Kardashians.

Kardashian is involved in the retail and fashion industries with her sisters, Kourtney and Kim. In 2016, Kardashian hosted her talk show, Kocktails with Khloe. She starred in and produced the health and fitness docu-series Revenge Body with Khloé Kardashian.
