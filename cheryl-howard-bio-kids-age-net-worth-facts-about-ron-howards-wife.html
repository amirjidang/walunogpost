Cheryl Howard Bio, Kids, Age, Net Worth, Facts About Ron Howards Wife

Cheryl Howard is a California-born actress who has made notable strides in the movie industry. Following her marriage to the accomplished filmmaker and actor, Ron Howard, who has achieved a well-decorated career, Cheryl has been cast in more than ten movies directed by her husband. Her acting intelligence can be seen in Willow, Night Shift, The Paper, Ransom, and others.

Aside from being famously known as the significant half of the Ron Howard, Cheryl Howard is also best recognised as the mother of two widely-known actresses, Bryce Dallas, and Paige Howard. Read on to find out more interesting facts about the wife of the multiple award-winning filmmaker, Ron Howard.

Cheryl Howard’s Biography (Age)

The gorgeous wife of Ron Howard was born in Glendale, California, the United States on the 23rd of December 1953. She is the daughter of Charles Alley and Vivian Schmid. Her father worked as an engineer and was a non-professional astronomer. She had two siblings. Concerning her academic endeavors, Cheryl Howard is an alumna of California State University where she acquired a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology. She further obtained MFA in Screenwriting.

Having been married to a movie director, Cheryl started featuring in some of her husband’s works such as Splash, Ransom, Night Shift, Willow, The Paper, among others. She has also played uncredited roles in other movies such as Grand Theft Auto, Apollo 13, Da Vinci Code and Angels & Demon.

Cheryl Howard’s Kids

The adept actress had a long term relationship with Ron Howard which began from their high school days. The loving relationship led to a nuptial bliss as the couple tied the knot in June 1975. Six years after their wedding, they welcomed their first child, a daughter named Bryce Dallas Howard. Later on, in 1985, Cheryl gave birth to twin daughters, Jocelyn Carlyle, and Paige Howard. The family further expanded by the arrival of their son, Reed Cross Howard in 1987.

Cheryl’s first daughter, Bryce is a prestigious actress who has received multiple accolades throughout her acting career and they include Hollywood Film Festival Award, Satellite Award, Screen Actors Guild Awards, Teen Choice Awards, among several others. The highlight of her acting career includes How the Grinch Stole Christmas, Book of Love, Spider-Man 3, The Twilight Saga: Eclipse, Rocketman, and many others.

Bryce’s younger sister, Paige is also following their family tradition of being an actor as she has played several television and film roles including Sue O’Malley in Adventureland, Jess in Cheesecake Casserole, Angela in 90210 and Dr. Julie in Happy.
