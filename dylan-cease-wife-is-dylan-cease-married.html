Dylan Cease Wife: Is Dylan Cease Married?

Dylan Edward Cease is an American professional baseball pitcher for the Chicago White Sox of Major League Baseball (MLB). He made his MLB debut in 2019.

Is Dylan Cease married? Cease is currently not married neither is he rumored to be involved in a relationship.

Cease attended Vanderbilt University on a college baseball scholarship to play for the Vanderbilt Commodores and during the summer seasons, he competed for Team Elite out of Winder, GA.

Cease excelled and his outstanding performnces earned him an invitation to the Perfect Game National Showcase and Perfect Game All-American Classic.

Cease was drafted by the Chicago Cubs in the sixth round. He signed with the Cubs, receiving a $1.5 million signing bonus, well above the $269,500 recommended at that slot.

On July 13, 2017 he was traded by the Cubs to the Chicago White Sox and he was assigned by The White Sox to the Kannapolis Intimidators of the Class A South Atlantic League.

In 22 total games between South Bend and Kannapolis, Cease posted a 1-10 record with a 3.28 ERA along with 126 strikeouts in a career high 93+1⁄3 innings pitched.
