10 Celebs That Pay Huge Bills For Their Security

Celebrities experience various luxuries in life, from expensive cars and homes to vacations and parties. While being an A-Lister has its advantages, it also means that they need to give up some of their freedom to ensure they are safe and secure. As celebrities gain a strong fanbase, there are chances of them getting a stalker or obsessed fan that might become a security threat. To ensure they are protected at all times, top celebrities spend millions of dollars on hiring security professionals. Many stories about stalkers stealing and breaking into homes have surfaced over the years, which makes security a top priority when heading out in public.

For singers likes Jennifer Lopez and Rihanna to actors and reality stars like Jennifer Aniston and the Kardashians, security is the most important aspect when dealing with obsessed fans. Let’s take a look at how much celebrities spend on their security.

Jennifer Aniston

America’s sweetheart, Jennifer Aniston, rose to prominence after starring in the iconic television sitcom Friends that ran for ten seasons. The star is also swarmed with fans who want to take a picture or get an autograph. Aniston is always guarded by men who ensure she remains unharmed. According to Living Alot, the actress spends $20,000 per month on her strong security detail.

Jennifer Lopez

Jennifer Lopez is one of the biggest Hollywood stars. Being in the constant spotlight, the actress/singer needs tough security protection. Lopez is guarded by The Force Protection Agency that hires bodyguards that are ex-cops or ex-military. The agency exclusively works with top celebrities like Lopez, who spends $42,000 every month to ensure that she and her children are protected.

Rihanna

Over the last few years, Rihanna has branched out from her musical career and become a top-notch businesswoman who created a billion-dollar empire. Rihanna is always spotted stepping outside with her security paid $500,000 every year for her protection. The singer is known to be on friendly terms with her bodyguards and is often spotted getting piggyback rides from them.

Harry Styles

A former member of One Direction who has had a blockbuster solo music career, Harry Styles is one of the biggest artists of his generation. Styles had a few scares in the last few years when he was stalked by a homeless man and mugged at gunpoint, as noted by The Things. The star then hired Mick Jagger’s bodyguard and additional security that cost him $1 million every year to follow him everywhere.

David And Victoria Beckham

David and Victoria Beckham have a lot more to protect than themselves. Along with their security, the couple ensures that enough protection is given to their four children: Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz, and Harper. The children are usually spotted being protected by bodyguard Bobby Rich, a part of the family for many years. The couple spends $1 million per year to ensure utmost security for themselves and their children.

Kim Kardashian

One of the biggest and most talked-about celebrity robberies occurred in 2016 when Kim Kardashian and her children were held at gunpoint in their Paris home. The reality star has publicly spoken about how security is important to protect her children and how the incident changed her life. She spends more than $100,000 a day on security totaling $3.5 million annually and additional money while attending fashion events.

Kylie Jenner

Kylie Jenner has become one of the most popular members of the Kardashian clan. After launching her skincare brand and products, Jenner becomes the owner of a billion-dollar brand. After the scare with Kim Kardashian, the entire family ensures security around themselves. As mentioned by The Sun, Jenner spends $400,000 per month on security. Her bodyguards also become a talking point in social media for their exquisite looks.

Angelina Jolie

Angelina Jolie is an iconic actress and a fierce mother. Raising six independent children, Jolie spends $2 million to ensure they are safe and secure at all times. A former bodyguard of the family revealed that there is a specific bodyguard assigned to each child to look after. The former bodyguards have also mentioned that they had a pleasant experience working for the family.

Lady Gaga

While already being an iconic singer, Lady Gaga’s acting career has skyrocketed in recent years. The mother of monsters ensures there is security around her and her dog. Her pet dog was kidnapped more recently while the dog walker was severely injured. Gaga has ensured maximum security around her home, paying $2.1 million annually for the services.

Beyoncé And Jay-Z

Celebrity power couple The Carters don’t compromise on their security. The couple is constantly in the public eye and has a collective net worth of over a billion dollars. Jay-Z has been particularly protective of the safety of Beyoncé and the three children they share. The couple has armored vehicles and 24-hour security that costs $4 million, as reported by The Richest.

Other celebrities that spend a fortune on their security are Daniel Radcliffe, Tom Cruise, and Johnny Depp. As the A-Listers come with high celebrity status, it is important to ensure that every security protocol is followed for their safety. With their growing presence on television and social media, security has become necessary.
