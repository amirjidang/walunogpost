50 Cent Speaks Out About Rumors Of His Unpaid Medical Bills Get Outta Here

50 Cent is at the top of his career and has a net worth declares he has far more money than he will ever spend in his lifetime. When it comes to success, he has climbed his way to the top, then catapulted himself even higher.

In fact, 50 Cent is so successful that not even a worldwide pandemic can get him down. He has smeared his success in the faces of people that have been affected by recent events, without hesitation. He owns a series of businesses, has made a huge footprint in the world of music, and has even released a self-help book which helps guide people out of difficult situations.

Interestingly, he has found himself in a tough situation now. His worldwide fans know that 50 Cent was famously shot 9 times and that made a shocking full recovery. What they may not know until just now, is the fact that the doctors claim he walked away without paying that life-saving medical bill.

So what does 50 Cent have to say about that? He says; "Get the F*#@ Outta Here"

50 Cent Won't Cough Up

There are two sides to every story, and in this case, these two sides are very divided. Curtis Jackson, aka 50 Cent is not even willing to give any attention to this doctor's requests. He's simply not paying the bill. He's making it seem as though the doctor is coming after him for his wealth and not to collect a legitimate bill that was outstanding. The doctor, Dr. Nader Paksima, claims that he performed life-saving measures to 50 Cent during the time that he required emergency treatment, and he claims that the rapper has the means to pay the bill, yet refuses to do so.

He simply won't cough it up, and the doctor has grown tired of waiting and asking nicely. Paksima has filed a lawsuit, citing unpaid legal bills and a claim worth just over $32, 000.  Paksima's legal team went on record to state that; "“My client provided very good medical care and [50 Cent] is in a position to pay for a very valuable service … He made a very nice recovery.”
