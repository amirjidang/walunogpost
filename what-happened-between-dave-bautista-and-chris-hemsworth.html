How Dave Bautista Really Feels About Chris Hemsworth

The bottom line is, Dave Bautista has the same opinion about Chris Hemsworth as many of his coworkers. Bautista reports that while filming Guardians of the Galaxy and Thor: Love and Thunder there were no issues with any of the star-studded characters. Bautista explained the atmosphere on the set was very cordial. He thinks that the executives of the MCU take personality into consideration when casting comic-book-based characters of their world.

Bautista describes Hemsworth as a "fun-loving Aussie." From Bautista's perspective, he considers the "very pleasant atmosphere" of the movie sets as something that was intentionally cultivated by everyone involved including Hemsworth. Bautista went on to say that he thinks Hemsworth is "hysterical" and they fit together very well.

But of course! Not only is Chris Hemsworth a funny guy, his coworkers, like vegan Oscar winner Natalie Portman, report that he is an extremely thoughtful person. His decision to not eat meat before a kiss scene with Portman in Thor: Love and Thunder, warmed the hearts of the internet.

Dave Bautista Has His Limits When It Comes To Certain Projects

David made it clear, he is not interested in being in projects that do not possess much meaning. For him, this decision means divorcing himself from the successful patterns of his WWE counterparts.

While Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson and John Cena achieved massive success, appearing in DC franchises and doing popcorn comedies, Bautista has no intention of appearing alongside them.

Fans certainly have their opinions regarding Bautista's acting chops. Some even believe he is a better performer than Dwayne Johnson. While the tweet does not have many likes or retweets, it is an inevitable comparison since both stars share a WWE history.

Moviegoers are talking about Bautista as a serious 'A-list' actor. His body of work is showing the actor possesses range and real acting skills. People definitely want more of Dave Bautista.
